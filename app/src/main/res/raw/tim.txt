Timothy John Betar (born April 8, 1990), better known by his online alias TimTheTatman, is an American YouTube exclusive streamer and internet personality. Betar started streaming on Twitch in 2012 and has amassed over 6.5 million followers since. Broadcasting games such as Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Overwatch, Fortnite, and World of Warcraft, the variety channel attracts thousands of viewers each day.
